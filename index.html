Unexpected Dutch

Amsterdam

Amsterdam. That is often one of the first things I hear when I say I live in The Netherlands. Often accompanied with assuming that that is where I live (nope!). True, it's a magnificant city, known by many. But The Netherlands is so much more than that.

Did you know about the (micro) deserts that can be found on a few rare locations in The Netherlands? Or the old stone graves that were build during the same time as the pyramids and Stonehenge?

On this page I'll try to show some of the "hidden" gems that may not be known so much outside of The Netherlands.
