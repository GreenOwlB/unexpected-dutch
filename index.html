Unexpected Dutch

Amsterdam

Amsterdam. That is often one of the first things I hear when I tell someone that I live in The Netherlands. Often accompanied with assuming that that is where I live (nope!). True, it's a magnificant city. But The Netherlands is so much more than that.

Did you know about the (micro) deserts that can be found on a few rare locations in The Netherlands? Or have you heard about the old stone graves that were build during the same time as the pyramids and Stonehenge?

On this page I'll show some of the "hidden" gems that may or may not be known so much outside of The Netherlands. And adding a bit of fun facts here and there. Stay tuned for more!

Aekingerzand

The Aekingerzand is part of a nature reserve in the Northern part of the Netherlands: the Drenths-Friese Wold. The area shows what part of the Netherlands actually is beneith the visible surface: a very large beach!
